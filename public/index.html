Who We Are

Our History

Established in 1985, Shift Key Mechanics started as a small family-owned garage with a passion for engines. Over four decades, we've grown into a premier automotive service center, servicing thousands of vehicles with the same dedication and care as day one.

Our founder, John "Shift" Key, believed that honesty and quality were the only tools a mechanic truly needed. Today, we uphold that legacy in every bolt we turn.

About Us

We are a team of ASE-certified mechanics dedicated to keeping your vehicle safe and reliable. We specialize in preventative maintenance and critical repairs, using state-of-the-art diagnostic tools.

At Shift Key Mechanics, we don't just fix cars; we build trust. No hidden fees, no unnecessary up-sells, just honest work at fair prices for our community.
